# Privacy Policy for Restriced Insta

Effective Date: 5. April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lia Kielack ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in relation to the Restriced Insta browser extension for Google Chrome.

1. No Data Collection

The Restricted Insta extension is designed with a "privacy-first" approach.
- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- We do not track your browsing history, search queries, or IP address.
- We do not sell or share any user data with third parties because we simply do not have access to it in the first place.

2. Use of Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the extension require access to ´*://*.instagram.com/*´ to edit the content of this webpage. These permissions are used locally on your device only. Any configuration settings or data processed by the extension remain within your local browser environment.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage users to check this page periodically for updates.
